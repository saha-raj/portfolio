About Me

I'm a computational scientist and visual storyteller with a background in physics, mathematical modeling / machine learning, and computer vision. I use data-driven and visual approaches to portray complex systems and communicate them clearly and intuitively.

My work spans education and real-world applications — designing interactive tools, building machine learning and computer vision systems, and creating visual narratives that combibe insights with artistic expression. I bring a systems mindset and interdisciplinary approach to help people understand and visualize complex data and systems.
