Visual Storytelling Through Computation

About Me

I'm a computational scientist and visual storyteller with a background in physics, data analysis, and computer vision. My work combines technical expertise with creative approaches to uncover stories hidden in data and communicate complex concepts visually.

With experience in building machine learning models, computer vision systems, and interactive visualizations, I create tools that help reveal patterns and insights that would otherwise remain invisible. My interdisciplinary background gives me unique perspectives on how to approach data-driven storytelling challenges.
